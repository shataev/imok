How it works

Three steps. Every day.

imok is designed to be invisible on good days — and essential when it matters.

1

You get a notification

Every day at your chosen time, imok sends a push notification to your phone. One tap to confirm you're OK.

2

Tap "I'm OK"

Open the app and tap the big green button. That's it. Takes less than 3 seconds. Your contacts are not disturbed.

3

Missed? They're notified

If you don't respond within your grace period, your trusted contacts are notified. No app needed on their end.

Features

Quietly powerful.

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

🕐

Your schedule, your time

Set your check-in time and grace period. Get a reminder if you forget. Miss it — and only then — your contacts hear.

✈️

Pause when travelling

Going on a trip? Pause check-ins for a date range. No accidental alerts. Resume automatically when you're back.

📱

No app for contacts

Your trusted contacts are notified when it matters. They don't need to install anything or create an account.

🔒

Private by design

We only store what's needed. No location tracking, no health data. PDPA compliant. Delete your account any time.

🌍

Works across timezones

Check-ins fire at your local time, wherever you are. Move to a new country — update your timezone in one tap.

📋

Full history

See your check-in history for the last 30 days. Confirmed, missed, paused — all in one clean view.
